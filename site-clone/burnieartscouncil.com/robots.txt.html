stARTs North-West is dedicated to artists, the arts, and culture in our community. 

 

In 2023, we celebrated 75 years of continuous operation, an extraordinary milestone that reflects the dedication of those who have nurtured and led this organisation for decades.

Our recent name change honours that legacy, whilst acknowledging that times have changed. 

stARTs North-West aims to support and represent the vibrant and contemporary activity of artists, arts organisations and cultural practitioners across the region.

JOIN UP! Membership is only $15. We would love to hear from you.

stARTs in March has been a celebration of arts and culture right across the North-West Coast with a month-long calendar of creative activities.

Over 60 events, workshops, gigs and performances were held. They were all brilliant, but of course all anyone wants to know is ‘Who is the Next Top Dog’?

Astra is the Next Top Dog.
