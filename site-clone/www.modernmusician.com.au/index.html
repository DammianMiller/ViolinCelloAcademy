Welcome to Modern Musician

Since 2008, Modern Musician has been Tasmania’s primary source for anything guitar, bass, keyboard, piano, drum & percussion, recording and live audio.

In 2017 we launched our backline & PA hire department, and in 2018 we’re proud to announce our band & orchestral division, catering to the needs of schools and students looking for the highest quality orchestral strings, brass, woodwind and percussion.

You can explore our brand new 300m2 showroom, have your instrument repaired by our in-store service department, have your drums re-headed and tuned, enjoy tech support from our knowledgeable team of specialists, all in your own time, whilst enjoying our complimentary off-street car parking.

Shop online anytime, drool over our guitar gallery, and enjoy up to 12 months interest free with Zip Money, as well as other payment options from zipPay & Afterpay.
